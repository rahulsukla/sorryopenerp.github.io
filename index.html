AGPL - License to fail

The provisions of AGPL make it mandatory for the source code to be presented in a downloadable link. The real question is, who is the user? The user can be your customer, who might turn into your competitor, he/she can be a rogue employee, a supplier looking to takeover your business or a competitor. What would you prefer, violating the license or sharing the source code? Well, even if you choose either one, both are legal.
