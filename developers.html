Using Floating Point Arithmetic for Accounting

OpenERP has the unique distinction of using floats instead of decimals for rounding values, which leads to unbalanced accounting. This feature has been continuously criticised since the inception of OpenERP, but in vain, as the creators of this ERP still continue to implement the same method.

Here's something handy for the OpenERP developers, who clearly forgot to attend CS 101 classes. And the developers who want to become computer scientists, well, you need to get your basics right, though we don’t see much hope for OpenERP developers in this field.

PS: Do not depend on the exactness of floating point arithmetic, even for apparently simple expressions! And don't bet your business on an ERP system that uses floating point arithmetic
